## 🌱 Interests

**Agents that do real work.** The AI I find interesting isn't chatbots — it's systems that take a messy, high-friction process and run it end-to-end: monitoring fragmented public data sources, matching tenders to companies, handling fiscal paperwork, explaining their own decisions. Everything on this profile orbits around that idea, from different angles:

**data & scraping pipelines** at scale · **multi-agent architectures** that earn their complexity · **semantic search & NLP** on messy real-world text · **evaluation & benchmarking** of AI agents · **risk modeling** (credit, operational) · **local-first LLMs** · shipping **complete products**, backend to UI
