## 🚀 About me

I build **AI systems that do real work** — from multi-agent pipelines and semantic search to production apps for Italian businesses.

- 🎓 **MSc in Data Science & Management** @ LUISS Business School · BSc in **Economics & Big Data** @ Roma Tre
- 📊 Worked as **Actuarial Analyst @ INAIL**, building predictive models for workplace injury risk (Python & R) — the project became the empirical basis of my bachelor's thesis
- 🏗️ I run [**MayAI**](https://mayai.it), where we build **AI that works the way Italian businesses do**: custom agents wired into the tools companies already use — certified email (PEC), e-invoicing, public tenders

I care about systems that are **measurable, reproducible and deployable**, not demos — and I believe learning by doing is the fastest path to impact.
